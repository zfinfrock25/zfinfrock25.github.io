About

Research shaped around instrumentation and impact

Based on public Argonne materials, Zou Finfrock focuses on using X-ray techniques to answer questions across multiple scientific domains, especially in biological and environmental research. The work emphasizes both method development and practical support for researchers using large-scale user facilities.

Public references also connect this work to spectroscopy programs, beamline support, and collaborative efforts such as eBERlight and structural biology capabilities at the Advanced Photon Source. Papers associated with the public scholar profile also show a broader record spanning crystallography, radioluminescent nanomaterials, environmental geochemistry, and electrochemical conversion research.
