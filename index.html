About

Building synchrotron programs around instrumentation and impact

Zou Finfrock is a synchrotron scientist and program leader with a record of advancing X-ray techniques, developing new beamline instrumentation, and growing user facility programs that support biological, environmental, and materials science communities.

The work spans commissioning new capabilities, leading multimillion-dollar initiatives, mentoring teams, and shaping next-generation synchrotron workflows that combine in situ and operando experiments, multimodal measurements, and AI/ML-enabled data analysis.

Highlights

What the work emphasizes

Instrumentation and commissioning

Designed and commissioned confocal XRF microscopy, X-ray excited optical luminescence, multi-analyzer HERFD, sub-micron focusing optics, and high-resolution spectroscopy concepts for synchrotron research.

Program development and funding

Secured major program support for user-facility science, renewed partner-user program support through multiple proposal cycles, and helped grow long-term synchrotron capabilities.

User communities and mentorship

Managed beamline operations, trained users, mentored junior scientists, and organized workshops that connect technical capabilities with real scientific communities.
